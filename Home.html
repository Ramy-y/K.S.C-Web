About us

Welcome to Hands for MENA We're an organization made up of students from varying backgrounds with a common interest in building a better world for less fortunate communities in the MENA region and beyond.

MENA stands for Middle East and North Africa which is a region of the world largely neglected and forgotten about by western society despite being responsible for many historic conflicts that prevent many communities in various MENA and greater MENA countries from having the same basic luxuries we often take for granted here in the west.

Through a team of passionate students and donations we hope to bring happiness and ease to children and their families in varying countries and communities via humanitarian aid in the form of clothes, toys, and food supplies.
